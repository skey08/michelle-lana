About Me

I am currently working as a senior visual designer at Navy Federal Credit Union in Vienna, VA where I craft accessible and inclusive product experiences for web and mobile applications.

Hi, my name is Michelle.

My job as a visual designer is to improve our users' lives and identify what it will take to make that happen. That means understanding their problems and looking past the solutions our customers first ask for. My goal is to find ways for everyone to participate in a shared experience, recognize mismatched interactions and create meaningful and useful experiences for all people.

Humble Beginnings

I started my career as a graphic designer after graduating from George Mason University in May 2000 with a solid background in print and illustration. With patience, perseverance, and inexhaustible dedication to my craft, I developed my own style. Over the years, I became involved in a range of corporate and freelance projects where I helped provide engaging user experiences/UI designs for web and mobile applications. Over the years, I worked closely with a variety of clients from mid-start-up firms to Fortune 500 organizations.
